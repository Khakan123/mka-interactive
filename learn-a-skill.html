Learn a New Skill

Below are some useful online links to courses that khuddam (and atfal) can do during this lockdown or self-isolation.

Free online courses

The Open University

The Open University has made available a number of free courses for this period of lock down. These can all be found at https://www.open.edu/openlearn/free-courses/full-catalogue

LinkedIn Learning

LinkedIn Learning has unlocked free online courses about working-from-home and how to stay productive in several languages. You don’t need to purchase license in order to watch these:

Remote Working: Setting Yourself and Your Teams Up for Success 

Or https://www.linkedin.com/learning/paths/remote-working-setting-yourself-and-your-teams-up-for-success?accountId=104&u=104&success=true&authUUID=j6dPrXvJTO2DmR6aoSg%2BMw%3D%3D

You can purchase monthly subscription for $29.99 per month in order to watch further courses.

Udemy

Udemy is the leading global marketplace for teaching and learning, connecting millions of students to the skills they need to succeed.

There are over 6,000 free online courses to choose from:

https://www.udemy.com/courses/search/?src=ukw&q=free

Vision2Learn

Offers free online professional qualification courses with certification

https://www.vision2learn.net/courses
